    • Street Genius

    Street Genius is an interactive outreach by Motolani Larodo Network that takes learning to the streets. We engage everyday people—especially youths—with fun general knowledge questions, rewarding correct answers with a gift of their choice from a variety of meaningful options.

    Whether in markets, parks, or neighborhoods, this initiative celebrates intelligence, encourages curiosity, and brings education closer to the community. It’s our way of proving that learning can happen anywhere—and that knowledge is worth celebrating.
