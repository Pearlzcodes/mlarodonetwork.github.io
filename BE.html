    • Basic Education

    This critical program is designed to educate children, parents, and educators on the realities of child sexual abuse. Delivered in schools and community centers, the sessions teach children how to recognize inappropriate behavior, speak up confidently, and seek help. Age-appropriate materials are used to ensure clarity, while caregivers and teachers are trained to detect signs of abuse and create safe environments. The goal is to protect children through awareness, advocacy, and early intervention.
