    • Responsible Daughters' Workshop

    This flagship program of Motolani Larodo Network is designed to shape the character, mindset, and future of teenagers, both male and female. It provides a safe, value-driven environment where young people are inspired to rise above peer pressure, societal confusion, and internal struggles. The workshop and summit focus on three major pillars:

    1. Discovering and Developing Your Gifts: Participants are guided to recognize their unique talents and potentials through self-awareness exercises, mentorship, and practical sessions. They are taught that their gifts are tools for impact and purpose, not just personal success. We help them align their natural abilities with long-term vision and godly values.
    2. Relationship with the Society: Young people are educated on how to interact responsibly with the world around them. Discussions focus on leadership, integrity, empathy, and social responsibility. We emphasize the importance of being agents of change—teenagers who model respect, contribute to community growth, and engage society with wisdom and maturity.
    3. Teenage Pregnancy and Sex: In a culture flooded with misinformation and unhealthy pressures, this segment delivers truth with love and clarity. Teens are taught the consequences of sexual activity outside God’s plan, the value of their bodies, and how to make informed, moral decisions. We create open conversations where questions are addressed with truths, medical facts, and emotional insight. Our goal is to equip them to walk in purity, confidence, and conviction.

    Through real-life stories, teaching, group discussions, and mentorship, this program continues to transform hundreds of teenagers into responsible, purpose-driven young men and women.
